## How Evolution Works

Your pet evolves into different characters based on care quality.
Two factors determine evolution:

### 1. Care Misses

The most important hidden parameter. Counted when:

| Condition | Grace Period |
|-----------|-------------|
| Hunger at 0, left unattended | ~15 min |
| Happiness at 0, left unattended | ~15 min |
| Illness left untreated | Immediate to hours |
| Poop left too long | 10-15 min |
| Didn't turn off lights after sleep | ~15 min |
| Ignored spoiled call | ~15 min |

### 2. Discipline Meter

+25% for each correct discipline. Maximum 100%.

</div>

<div class="section" markdown="1">

## Evolution Tiers

Care misses and discipline meter determine evolution tier.

| Tier | Condition | Result |
|------|-----------|--------|
| ⭐ Tier 1 | 0-1 misses + 100% discipline | Best character |
| ⭐ Tier 2 | 1-2 misses + 75% discipline | Good character |
| Tier 3 | 3-5 misses + 50% discipline | Average character |
| Tier 4 | 6+ misses | Poor character |

## Raising Tips

### How to Reach Tier 1

1. **Check hunger & happiness often** — Care before they reach zero
2. **Clean poop immediately** — Neglect causes illness
3. **Master discipline timing** — Only discipline when spoiled
4. **Turn off lights quickly** — Within 15 minutes of bedtime
5. **Go easy on snacks** — Obesity hurts evolution

### Preventing Death

| Cause | Prevention |
|-------|------------|
| Starvation | Don't leave hunger at 0 for hours |
| Disease | Treat illness within 12 hours |
| Accumulated damage | Reduce care misses to extend lifespan |
| Old age | Unavoidable even with perfect care (7-25 days) |

</div>

<div class="section" markdown="1">

## Lifespan

Care quality determines lifespan: **approximately 7-25 days**.

- Perfect care: up to 25 days
- Average care: ~14 days
- Neglected: ~7 days

Generational bonuses accumulate over time. When your pet passes on, a new egg begins a new adventure.
