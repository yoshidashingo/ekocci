## Life Cycle

Your pet hatches from an egg and grows through stages based on your care.

| Stage | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| 🥚 Egg | ~5 min | Wobbles on screen, hatches automatically |
| 👶 Baby | ~1 hour | Needs constant care. Cannot die at this stage |
| 🧒 Child | ~24 hours | Mini games unlocked. Frequent care needed |
| 🧑‍🎤 Teen | ~2-3 days | Evolution branches begin |
| 🧑 Adult | Until end | Final form. Character is set |
| 👴 Senior | Late adult | Only long-lived pets reach this stage |

<div class="pixel-divider"></div>

## Built for Apple Watch

- **Complications** — Check pet status from your watch face
- **Taptic Engine** — Feel feeding, evolution, and games through haptics
- **Digital Crown** — Scroll menus and play mini games
- **Always-On Display** — Your pet is always with you
